COVID-19 Protocol

COVID-19 is still very much a problem, and we unfortunately do not anticipate it no longer being a problem by the time of the wedding. To protect ourselves and our guests, we will be taking the following protocols throughout the weekend:


  • You must be vaccinated to attend any portion of the wedding weekend.
  • Whenever you arrive at the lodge, you will be asked to take a rapid COVID-19 test, which we will provide.
    • Anyone who tests positive will only be able to participate in the outdoor portions of the weekend and must wear an N95 or KN95 mask, which we will provide.
    • If you are staying at the lodge and test positive, we will help you find alternative accommodations for the weekend.
  • We will not be requiring masks if you test negative, but you are welcome to wear one if that makes you more comfortable.
