Places to Stay

Due to the small size of our wedding, we are unable to secure discounted hotel prices for guests not staying at the lodge.
However, we have found a number of available places to stay at various price points.
Here are some of the better available options, from least to most expensive:


Bunkhouse In the Pines
5-star, one-bedroom (2 guests) via Airbnb. Rental includes the entire bunkhouse.
Pet-friendly
20 minutes from the venue
~$100 per night

Eagle Fire Lodge and Cabins
Well-rated cabins and suites of varying sizes
Pet-friendly (in most suites)
25 minutes from the venue
$125-$175 per night

Microtel Inn & Suites
Basic hotel with varying room sizes
No pets allowed
25 minutes from the venue
$150-$200 per night

Rampant Range Resort
Highly rated bed & breakfast with suites of varying sizes
One pet-friendly suite available, costs extra
25 minutes from the venue
$200-$300 per night

Pikes Peak Resort
A group of 5 highly rated cabins of varying sizes
No pets allowed
5 minutes from the venue
$350-$400 per night

If none of these suit your needs, we recommend checking out the many available Airbnbs in the area, which start at $75 per night.
There are also several hotels available in Colorado Springs, approximately 50 minutes from the venue.
