Transportation

The lodge is about 25 minutes outside the nearest town, Woodland Park.
A decent portion of the drive to the lodge from Woodland Park is along an unpaved road that is steep and narrow at times.


Getting to Woodland Park:
(we recommend going down to Colorado Springs and then up to Woodland Park)

  • Rent a car ($40-$60/day + gas)
    If you do not live in the Denver area, we highly recommend renting a car.
    Given the remote location of the lodge, this will likely be the easiest and most cost-effective way to get around for the weekend.
    There is plenty of parking at the lodge.
    • Total round-trip cost: $120-$180
    • One-way travel time from Denver: 2 hours
  • Fly into Colorado Springs airport ($100-$150 more than a flight to Denver) + Lyft/Uber to Woodland Park ($50-$75)
    • Total round-trip cost: $200-$300
    • One-way travel time from Colorado Springs: 45 minutes
  • Take Groome Transportation to Colorado Springs ($50) + Lyft/Uber to Woodland Park ($50-$75)
    • Total round-trip cost: $200-$250
    • One-way travel time from Denver: 3 hours

Getting from Woodland Park to the lodge:

  • If you are comfortable driving along an unpaved road, you can use your rented car to drive yourself to and from the lodge.
    Please consider you would be driving along the road in the dark after the ceremony and dinner on Saturday.
  • If you are uncomfortable driving along an unpaved road, you may be able to find an Uber/Lyft from downtown Woodland Park.
    Be prepared for a wait. You may want to reserve it ahead of time to ensure there is a car available.
  • On the afternoon of the ceremony (10/8), a shuttle will be available to take you to and from the lodge if you are staying at one of the recommended hotels.
