Transportation

The lodge is about 25 minutes outside the nearest town, Woodland Park.
A decent portion of the drive to the lodge from Woodland Park is along an unpaved road that is steep and narrow at times.
If you are staying at the lodge or in Woodland Park, we recommend driving down to Colorado Springs and approaching from the south.



Getting to Woodland Park

  • Rent a car ($40-$60/day + gas). Given the remote location of the lodge, this is the easiest and most cost-effective way to get around.
    • Total round-trip cost: $120-$180
    • One-way travel time from Denver: 2 hours

  • Fly into CO Springs airport ($100-$150 more than flying to Denver) + Lyft/Uber to Woodland Park ($50-$75)
    • Total round-trip cost: $200-$300
    • One-way travel time from Colorado Springs: 45 minutes

  • Take Groome Transportation to Colorado Springs ($50) + Lyft/Uber to Woodland Park ($50-$75)
    • Total round-trip cost: $200-$250
    • One-way travel time from Denver: 3 hours


Getting from Woodland Park to the lodge

  • On the afternoon of the ceremony (10/8), a shuttle will be available to take you to and from the lodge if you are staying at one of the recommended hotels.
  • Any other time, you can drive yourself to and from the lodge if you are comfortable driving along an unpaved road. Otherise, you may be able to find an Uber/Lyft from downtown Woodland Park. Be prepared for a wait. You may want to reserve it ahead of time to ensure there is a car available.
